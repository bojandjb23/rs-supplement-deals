01 — Logo & Icon Mark

The mark

A bold, geometric "S" formed from two stacked arcs — reading simultaneously as a supplement capsule, a currency symbol, and a downward-trending price. Paired with the wordmark "suplementi.deals" in Montserrat ExtraBold.

08 — Voice & Tone

How we communicate

suplementi.deals speaks like a knowledgeable friend who found a great deal and wants to share it — not like a marketing department.

✓ Do
Ušteda: 2.700 RSD — concrete number
Ažurirano pre 2 sata — specific recency
45% popust — lead with the discount
15 prodavnica — precise, not "many"
Bez spama. Odjavi se kad god. — honest
Kupi → — direct, single action
✗ Don't
Neverovatna ušteda! — vague superlatives
Redovno ažuriramo — passive non-commitment
Velika akcija na proteine — no number = no trust
Mnoge prodavnice — imprecise
Pretplati se na newsletter — corporate-feel
Klikni ovde za detalje — weak, no direction

Brand personality axes

Trustworthy Flashy
Practical Inspirational
Concise Verbose
Data-first Emotion-first
